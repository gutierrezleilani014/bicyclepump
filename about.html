About.

Riding a bike is freedom. It's hearing the wheels spin on a quiet road, getting stoked hitting a cool trail, or just feeling good getting where you're going using your own power. Some people ride to work, others race, explore, or just live on their bike.

No matter where you go, your bike works best when your tires have enough air.

That's why we do what we do. We make bike pumps and tools so riders feel confident and can depend on their gear, so they don't have to sweat if their tires are good. For us, a pump isn't just some add-on – it's like your secret teammate, linking your bike to the road.

How We Got Started

It all started when we noticed something was wrong. Too many riders were wrestling with bad pumps – handles breaking, gauges lying, hoses cracking, and air leaking everywhere. A pump is super important, and it should never let you down.

So, we decided to make pumping tires easy and reliable for every rider.

We checked out how people really use pumps – in garages, on trails, before races, and on the way to work. We saw the problems: valve heads that didn't fit, pumps that took forever, tiny pumps that wore you out, and big pumps that could hit high pressure but weren't tough enough.

That's how we came up with our idea: make tools that last, work every time, and help riders get out there. That's what we still believe today.

What We Want to Do

Our goal is simple: keep riders rolling without worry.

We don't just make pumps; we build trust. When you pump, the handle, the valve head, and the gauge should tell you that you're ready to go.

We think good tire pressure is key to being safe, riding well, and having fun. If we can get rid of bad equipment, riders can just focus on riding.

What We Believe

These ideas guide what we make and how we treat the cycling crowd:

Always Dependable – The pump should just work, every time. That's what we promise.

Fits Everything – Riders have all kinds of bikes, valves, and tires. Our stuff is made to fit easily, so no one is left out.

Easy to Use – Simple locks and comfy grips make pumping fast and easy.

Right on the Money – Good pressure means better riding. Our pumps give you the right numbers, whether it's 20 psi for a fat tire or 120 psi for a road bike.

Tough and Long-Lasting – Our pumps are built to take a beating, whether you ride to work or ride all day.

Part of the Group – Riding bikes is about people as much as bikes. We like being part of the community and helping each other out.

Looking Out for the Planet – We try to waste less, be more efficient, and make products that last longer, so you don't have to buy new ones all the time.

What We Sell

We make pumps for all riders and situations:

Floor Pumps: For your garage, made to pump fast with a stable base and a good gauge.

Mini Pumps: Small and easy to carry, for fixing flats on the road.

CO₂ Inflators: Super light and fast, great for racers or anyone who needs a quick fix.

Foot Pumps: Small enough to take with you but still stable.

Electric Pumps: Just push a button for easy pumping.

Tubeless Stuff: Big volume pumps for seating tubeless tires fast.

We have what you need, whether you ride a road bike, mountain bike, commuter, gravel bike, or fat bike.
